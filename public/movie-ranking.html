Into The Badlands

Into the Badlands is an American television series that aired on AMC from 2015 to 2019. Created by Alfred Gough and Miles Millar, the series is known for its distinctive blend of martial arts action, post-apocalyptic setting, and intricate storytelling. This is set in a dystopian future where society has collapsed, and the world is ruled by feudal barons. Guns are banned, and combat is conducted with martial arts and melee weapons. The series follows the journey of a skilled warrior named Sunny and a young boy named M.K. as they navigate the dangerous and divided territories known as the Badlands.

Spider-Man (Homecoming)

Spider-Man: Homecoming is a 2017 superhero film based on the Marvel Comics character Spider-Man. It is the second reboot of the Spider-Man film franchise and the sixteenth film in the Marvel Cinematic Universe (MCU). Directed by Jon Watts, the film stars Tom Holland as the titular character. The film follows Peter Parker, a high school student in New York City who has recently been recruited by Tony Stark (Iron Man) to join the Avengers. Balancing his life as a regular teenager with his duties as Spider-Man, Peter faces his biggest challenge yet when he uncovers a dangerous plot by a villain named Adrian Toomes, also known as the Vulture.
